About Us

Welcome to CamSkript , formerly known as Smile skill Acquisition Centre ,a sanctuary for personal growth, healing, and transformation. At the core of our mission is a passionate commitment to empower individuals on their journey towards a more fulfilling and balanced life.
We are a team of dedicated professionals, each with a unique blend of expertise, experience, and a shared dedication to your well-being. Our counselors, educators, and trainers are united by the common goal of helping you unlock your full potential.

We offer Services

Remedial Training

We believe that every obstacle can become a stepping stone. Our remedial training programs help you overcome difficulties in learning, be it academic or practical, allowing you to reach your goals.

Psychological Training (TA & NLP)

Our expert psychological training in Transactional Analysis (TA) and Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) equips you with powerful tools for self-awareness, communication, and personal transformation.

Hypnotisam/Hypnotherapy

Hypnotism, is a trance-like state of focused attention, heightened suggestibility, and deep relaxation. During hypnosis, a person is more open to suggestions and may experience changes in perception, memory, and behavior. It's often used for therapeutic purposes, entertainment, and self-improvement.

Pre-marital Counseling

Before you embark on the beautiful journey of marriage, we offer pre-marital counseling to help you and your partner build a strong foundation, enhancing understanding and communication.

Skill Improvement Programs

We're committed to your continuous growth. Our skill improvement programs cover a wide range of areas, from communication skills to leadership development, ensuring you're always evolving.
